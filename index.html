About me

I am someone who enjoys solving problems and building things that actually work. My technical background gives me the ability to understand how systems function, which helps me think through challenges from multiple angles. I like to dig into what people need and find practical ways to deliver it.

One thing I have learned is that good communication makes everything easier.
